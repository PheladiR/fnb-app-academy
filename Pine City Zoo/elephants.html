ELEPHANTS

Elephants are large mammals of the family Elephantidae and the order Proboscidea. Two species are traditionally recognised, the African elephant (Loxodonta africana) and the Asian elephant (Elephas maximus)...

All elephants have several distinctive features, the most notable of which is a long trunk or proboscis...

Elephants are herbivorous and can be found in different habitats including savannahs, forests, deserts and marshes.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM
